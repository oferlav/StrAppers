Design Partnership Proposal

Hire by Skill,
not by CV.

A new standard for role-based hiring, where every position has a precise Skill definition and every candidate is matched against it with evidence.

The challenge

The problem isn't the interview.
It's who you invite to it.

  • CVs tell you where someone worked, not whether they can actually do the job you need done.
  • The real cost is the hours your team spends screening, meeting, and assessing people who weren't the right fit from the start.
  • By the time you find out a candidate isn't skilled for your specific role, you've already invested weeks of your team's time.
Interviews and assessments are valuable, but only when you're already talking to the right people. That's exactly what Skill-in solves.

The concept

Introducing the
Skill.

A Skill is a structured, AI-native definition of what great looks like for a specific position at your company.

Built from how your best people actually work, not from a generic job description.

Used directly by AI to match and assess candidates against your standard, not a recruiter's gut feel.

Privacy

Zero personal data
in the output.

Your team's data is used to identify patterns, not to profile individuals. The Skill contains no names, no identifiers, and no personal information of any kind.

🔒
No personal data in output

The Skill is a behavioral pattern. No names, emails, or identifiers. It cannot be linked to any individual.

⚡
Raw data is ephemeral

Individual signals are processed and deleted immediately after synthesis. Only the aggregated pattern survives.

👁
Full employee transparency

Employees receive a clear notice explaining what signal categories are analyzed. Participation is consensual.

The output is a pattern, not a profile. No individual can ever be identified from it.
