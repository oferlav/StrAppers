## Slide 3 — What Skill-in Does

**Skill-in turns your top performers into a hiring blueprint.**

1. **Reads** activity from your existing tools — Jira, GitHub, Slack
2. **Scores** each dimension with AI: delivery, code quality, communication
3. **Builds** your Skillset — a precise, role-specific standard anchored to your team
4. **Matches** it against a pipeline of students who worked on real projects

You don't write a rubric. You don't define "good."
Your best people already did that — we just measure it.

## Slide 4 — How It Works (3 Steps)

### Step 1 — Connect (5 minutes)
Install the Skill-in plugin in your Claude Code environment.
Point it at Jira, GitHub, and Slack. No new accounts. No migrations.

### Step 2 — Generate Your Skillset (30 minutes)
Pick 3–5 top performers and a date range.
Run `/generate-skillset`.
Review the AI-scored dimensions. Add your own ratings. Set weights.
Done.

### Step 3 — Get Matched
Skill-in compares your Skillset against its student pipeline.
When a candidate crosses your threshold — you get an alert.
No sourcing. No cold outreach. No resume screening.

## Slide 5 — What Data Is Used

**Only what already exists. Nothing new to create.**

| Source | What we read | What we score |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Jira | Task completion, story points, due dates | Delivery & velocity |
| GitHub | Commits, PRs, code review comments | Code quality & consistency |

**What we don't read:**
- Message content or text
- Personal files or documents

> You control the scope. You choose the employees. You review the output before anything is submitted.

## Slide 6 — Privacy & Security

**Your data never reaches Skill-in. Here's exactly what happens.**

```
Your Jira / GitHub / Slack
         ↓
   Your machine (local processing)
         ↓
   AI scores only  ──────────────→  Skill-in
   (no names, no content)
```

**What stays on your machine:** all raw activity data, all employee names, all message content.

**What Skill-in receives:** anonymized, aggregated scores. Numbers only. No employee can be identified from what we receive.

**What goes to Anthropic (Claude AI):** activity metadata — task counts, word counts, commit frequencies. No message content. Covered by your existing Anthropic enterprise agreement.

> You can inspect the exact JSON payload before it's submitted. Nothing is sent without your review.
